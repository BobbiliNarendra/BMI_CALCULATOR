Hydration and Weight Loss: The Water Advantage

Published on May 18, 2023 • 4 min read

Proper hydration can increase fat burning, reduce water retention, and help you feel fuller longer. Even mild dehydration of just 1-2% can significantly impact metabolism and cognitive function.

The Science of Water and Metabolism

Drinking 500ml of water increases metabolic rate by 30% for 30-40 minutes. This "water-induced thermogenesis" could burn an extra 100 calories daily.

Hydration Tips for Weight Loss

  • Drink 1 glass upon waking to activate organs
  • Consume 500ml 30 minutes before meals to reduce appetite
  • Add lemon or cucumber for flavor and detox benefits
  • Monitor urine color (pale yellow = ideal)
